About Me

How I became interested in AI Engineering

My dad is an IT technician, and I found it very interesting to tinker with computers with him growing up. Once, he even bought me a commemorative edition modular router, which was like a practical Lego set. From then on, I developed a strong interest in information technology, both hardware and software. The development and popularization of artificial intelligence in recent years has made me aware of future trends, and driven by my passion and the tide of the times, I chose AI Engineering as my major.
